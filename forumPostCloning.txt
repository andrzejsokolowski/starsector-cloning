[center][b]
Ever installed Astral Ascension but found you would need to wait years for a size 9 colony in the first place?
Ever wanted to see those cool multipliers from having a [i]Fractal Ecumenopolis[/i] — whatever that is, because you've never seen it without using console commands?
Ever installed Vulpoids and saw your celebrities produce 0.6% growth on a size 8?

Well, look no more.

Adds a three-tier cloning industry that massively boosts population growth — at a cost to stability.

[color=red]Requires [url=https://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=21022.0][u]LunaLib[/u][/url].[/color]

[td][hr][center][size=12pt][b]Industry Chain[/b][/size][/center][hr]
[list]
[li]Adds a new industry available from the start, occupying an industry slot.[/li]
[li]Three upgrade tiers, each producing more population growth than the last:
[list]
[li][b]Clone Vat Experiments[/b] — entry-level, produces some population growth.[/li]
[li][b]Cloning Industry[/b] — upgrade, produces more population growth.[/li]
[li][b]Cloning Megafactory[/b] — top tier, produces a large amount of population growth.[/li]
[/list][/li]
[li]Growth scales with colony size when an Alpha or Omega core is installed.[/li]
[li]All tiers apply a stability penalty that scales with colony size — there are consequences for cutting corners on biology.[/li]
[/list]

[hr][center][size=12pt][b]AI Core Effects[/b][/size][/center][hr]
[list]
[li][b]Gamma core[/b] — reduces upkeep. Cloning is expensive; every bit helps.[/li]
[li][b]Beta core[/b] — reduces upkeep and produces crew and marines alongside population growth.[/li]
[li][b]Alpha core[/b] — reduces upkeep, produces crew and marines, and growth now scales with colony size.[/li]
[li][b]Omega core[/b] [i](Astral Ascension)[/i] — all Alpha benefits, plus doubled colony size scaling. Expect extremely large numbers. Go wild.[/li]
[/list]

[hr][center][size=12pt][b]Configuration[/b][/size][/center][hr]
[list]
[li]Uses [url=https://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=21022.0]LunaLib[/url] for in-game settings.[/li]
[li]Configurable values: base growth per tier, colony size scaling factor, and a global multiplier.[/li]
[li]Players without LunaLib will use the built-in defaults.[/li]
[/list]

Definitely yes, until I manage to balance it somehow. And I mean [i]really[/i] overpowered — hitting over a thousand growth points on a size 7 colony is entirely possible right now.
